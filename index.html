Waterfalls in India

Explore the Beautiful Waterfalls of India

Discover India's most breathtaking waterfalls!

Nohkalikai Falls

Located in Meghalaya, this is one of the tallest waterfalls in India.

Take a flight to Shillong and then travel by road to reach the falls.

Dudhsagar Falls

Situated on the Goa-Karnataka border, it's known for its beauty.

Travel by train to Dudhsagar station or take a road trip through the sanctuary.

Jog Falls

Located in Karnataka, Jog Falls is one of the highest waterfalls in India.

Take a train to Sagar and travel by road to the falls.
